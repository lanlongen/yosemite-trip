Starting point · your base in the Valley

1Yosemite Valley Lodge

Kick off in the Valley with a driving break — gift shop, restrooms, and coffee before the climb. El Capitan towers right overhead. Stretch your legs, top up snacks, then point the car toward Wawona Road.

📍 Open in Google Maps
≈ 10 min from the Lodge

2Tunnel View

The postcard shot of Yosemite — El Capitan, Bridalveil Fall, and Half Dome framed in one sweep, straight from the car park. A quick drive-up viewpoint you can hit on the way up to Glacier Point or save for the drive back down.

📍 Open in Google Maps
🛣️ Glacier Point Road leg ≈ 45–60 min up a winding mountain road — the next three stops are all up here, close together.
≈ 50 min from Tunnel View (up Glacier Point Rd)

3Glacier Point

The big one. A short paved walk (~20 min round trip) opens onto a jaw-dropping panorama of Half Dome, the Valley floor far below, and the high country beyond. Easy underfoot and worth every step.

📍 Open in Google Maps
≈ 5 min from Glacier Point (just back down the road)

4Washburn Point

A drive-up viewpoint a minute below Glacier Point, with arguably the best straight-on profile of Half Dome and a peek at the Merced River's waterfalls. No walking required — pull over, look up, snap away.

📍 Open in Google Maps
≈ 10 min drive from Washburn Pt to the trailhead 🥾 For the fit hiker

5Taft Point

From the Sentinel Dome / Taft Point trailhead it's about 1.1 mi each way to a thrilling clifftop with the famous fissures and a guardrail over a sheer drop. The payoff is huge, but it's a real walk — best for the hikers in the group.
